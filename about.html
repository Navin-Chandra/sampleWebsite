Insights

BHANDERHATI B.M. INSTITUTION was established in 1894 and it is managed by the Department of Education. It is located in Rural area. It is located in DHANIAKHALI block of HUGLI district of West Bengal. The school consists of Grades from 5 to 12. The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section. The school is not using school building as a shift-school. Bengali is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April. The school has Government building. It has got 14 classrooms for instructional purposes. All the classrooms are in good condition. It has 2 other rooms for non-teaching activities. The school has a separate room for Head master/Teacher. The school has Pucca boundary wall. The school has electricity connection. The source of Drinking Water in the school is Tap Water and it is functional. The school has 1 boys toilet and 1 girls toilet and both are functional. The school has a playground. The school has a library which consist of 3000 books. The school does not need ramp for disabled children to access classrooms. The school has 10 computers for teaching and learning purposes and all are functional. The school is having a computer aided learning lab. The school provides mid-day meal which is prepared in the school premises.
